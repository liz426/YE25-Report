# Whatnot 2026 Trend Report - City Microsites

## Overview

This project contains five city-specific microsites showcasing trend insights from the Whatnot 2026 Complete Trend Report. Each microsite highlights local trends across Fashion, Toys & Collectibles, Food & Drink, Beauty, and Sports Cards.

## Cities Covered

1. **Chicago** - The Windy City's blend of Y2K nostalgia and cozy pragmatism
2. **Phoenix** - Desert trends focused on breathability and function
3. **Atlanta** - The South's cultural capital embracing cozy maximalism
4. **Dallas** - Big Texas energy with Western touches and luxury aspirations
5. **Miami** - Magic City's hot, hyped, vacation-ready aesthetic

## Getting Started

### Viewing Locally

1. Clone this repository
2. Navigate to the `microsites` directory
3. Open `index.html` in your web browser

### Deployment

These static HTML pages can be deployed to any web hosting service:

- **GitHub Pages** - Push to a `gh-pages` branch
- **Netlify** - Drag and drop the `microsites` folder
- **Vercel** - Connect your repository
- **AWS S3** - Upload as static website

## Data Source

All insights are derived from the **Whatnot 2026 Complete Trend Report**, covering year-over-year growth from 2024 to 2025 across five major U.S. markets.

## Key Insights

### National Trends

- **Plus-Size Fashion Revolution** - Triple/quadruple-digit growth across all cities
- **Popmart's Blind Box Takeover** - 14-24% toy market share
- **The Great Candy Takeover** - 20-33% of food sales
- **Fragrance Dethroned Makeup** - 2,100-3,800% YoY growth
- **NFL Overtakes Basketball** - 2.4-7.8 percentage point gains
- **Hometown Heroes Rule** - Local teams became #1 collecting category

### City Highlights

- **Chicago** - Blind box capital (18.9% Popmart share)
- **Phoenix** - Singles collecting capital (36.4% share)
- **Atlanta** - Fastest sports card growth (+155% YoY)
- **Dallas** - Candy capital (33% of food sales)
- **Miami** - Skincare capital (12.0% beauty share)
